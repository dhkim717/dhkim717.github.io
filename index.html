Welcome !

I am Donghoon Kim and an Associate Professor in Department of Computer Science at Arkansas State University.

My research interests include security for cloud computing and cyber-physical systems (CPS), parallel and high-performance computing, and machine learning. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science at North Carolina State University under the supervision of Dr. Mladen A. Vouk. Prior to my doctoral studies, I received my M.S. from Auburn University, AL, USA and worked at Samsung Electronics in the Republic of Korea.

Openings: I am looking for multiple self-motivated graduate (MS degree) or undergraduate students. If you are interested, please email me your resume.
